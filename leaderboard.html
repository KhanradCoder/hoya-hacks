eD Score Calculation

The eD score is calculated using a comprehensive approach to ensure fairness and accuracy. All data collected on utilities such as chilled water and electricity usage are standardized by computing the z-score, making eD always relative. The z-score is then multiplied by the Metric Tons of Carbon Dioxide Equivalents for each utility and summed to create a preliminary score.
